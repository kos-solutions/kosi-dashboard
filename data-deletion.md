# Data Deletion Instructions - KOSI

**How to Request Deletion of Your Personal Data**

---

## Your Right to Data Deletion

Under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and other privacy laws, you have the right to request complete deletion of all your personal data from KOSI ("Right to be Forgotten").

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently delete:

✅ **Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (hashed)
- Account creation date
- Subscription history

✅ **Child Profile Information:**
- Child's first name
- Age range
- Language preference

✅ **Voice Recordings:**
- Parent voice recordings (for voice cloning)
- Voice model generated by ElevenLabs

✅ **Usage Data:**
- Story transcripts
- Game scores and progress
- Activity logs
- Time spent in app

✅ **Device Information:**
- Device pairings
- Device identifiers
- App settings

---

## What Happens After Deletion?

### Immediate Effects:
- You will lose access to your account immediately
- All PRO subscription benefits end (no refunds for partial periods)
- The child's tablet will be unpaired
- Parent dashboard access is revoked

### Within 48 Hours:
- You will receive an email confirmation of deletion request
- Your data is marked for deletion in our systems

### Within 30 Days:
- All personal data is permanently deleted from our active systems
- Backup copies are removed
- Third-party processors (OpenAI, ElevenLabs) are notified to delete your data

### Permanent:
- Data cannot be recovered after 30 days
- You can create a new account, but it will not have any previous data

---

## How to Request Data Deletion

### Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open KOSI app
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Tap **Account**
4. Scroll down to **Delete Account**
5. Tap **Delete My Account and All Data**
6. Enter your password to confirm
7. Tap **Permanently Delete**

✅ **Instant:** Data deletion begins immediately

---

### Method 2: Email Request

1. Send an email to: **privacy@kosiapp.com**
2. **Subject:** Data Deletion Request
3. **Include in your email:**
   - Your registered email address
   - Your child's first name (for verification)
   - Reason for deletion (optional)

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Dear KOSI Privacy Team,

I request the complete deletion of all my personal data associated with my KOSI account.

Account Email: [your email]
Child Name: [child's first name for verification]

I understand that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

✅ **Timeline:** We will respond within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days

---

### Method 3: Parent Dashboard

1. Go to https://dashboard.kosiapp.com
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Click **Settings** (top right)
4. Click **Account Management**
5. Click **Delete Account and All Data**
6. Enter your password to confirm
7. Click **Permanently Delete**

✅ **Instant:** Data deletion begins immediately

---

## Verification Process

To protect your account from unauthorized deletion:

1. **Email Verification:** We may send a confirmation email to verify your identity
2. **Password Confirmation:** You must enter your current password
3. **Security Check:** For high-value accounts (Lifetime PRO), we may require additional verification

---

## Data Retention for Legal Compliance

### What We Keep (Minimal):
For legal and accounting purposes, we may retain minimal anonymized data:
- Transaction records (for tax/accounting): 7 years (legally required)
- Abuse/fraud logs: Up to 1 year (for security)

This data is:
- Completely anonymized (no link to your identity)
- Not accessible to you or anyone else
- Used only for legal compliance, not marketing or analytics

### What We Don't Keep:
- Voice recordings (deleted immediately)
- Story transcripts (deleted immediately)
- Personal identifiers (email, name, etc.) (deleted immediately)
- Usage patterns (deleted immediately)

---

## Third-Party Data Deletion

We will request deletion of your data from our third-party processors:

### OpenAI:
- Story generation prompts
- Speech transcriptions (already deleted immediately, not stored)
- Moderation logs

**Timeline:** OpenAI retains API logs for 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deletes automatically

### ElevenLabs:
- Parent voice recordings
- Generated voice model

**Timeline:** We request immediate deletion; ElevenLabs confirms within 14 days

### Supabase (Database):
- All database records

**Timeline:** Deleted within 24 hours, backup copies within 30 days

---

## Subscription Cancellation

### Active Subscriptions:
- Monthly/Annual subscriptions are canceled immediately (no refunds for partial periods)
- Lifetime PRO: No refunds (this is a one-time purchase)

### Google Play Refunds:
- Refund policies are governed by Google Play
- Contact Google Play Support for refund requests: https://support.google.com/googleplay

---

## Alternative: Data Export (Before Deletion)

If you want a copy of your data before deletion:

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Export My Data**
2. Or email privacy@kosiapp.com with subject "Data Export Request"
3. We will provide a JSON/CSV file with:
   - Account information
   - Story transcripts
   - Usage statistics
   - Voice recording metadata (not the recordings themselves)

**Timeline:** Data export provided within 7 days

---

## Re-Activation After Deletion

### Can I Reactivate?
**NO.** Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Creating a New Account:
You can create a new account with the same email, but:
- It will be treated as a completely new account
- No previous data will be restored
- No PRO benefits will transfer (unless you had Lifetime PRO and request restoration within 30 days of deletion)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does deletion take?
**A:** Immediate access revocation; complete deletion within 30 days.

### Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?
**A:** No. Deletion is permanent.

### Q: Will I get a refund for my PRO subscription?
**A:** No refunds for partial periods (per Google Play policy).

### Q: What if I change my mind?
**A:** You have 48 hours to cancel the deletion request by contacting support@kosiapp.com.

### Q: Will my child's tablet still work?
**A:** No. The tablet will be unpaired and the app will require a new account to function.

### Q: Can I delete just my voice recording, not my entire account?
**A:** Yes! Go to Settings → Voce Părinte → Delete Voice Recording.

### Q: What happens to my stories?
**A:** All story transcripts are permanently deleted. They cannot be recovered.

### Q: Is my data sold before deletion?
**A:** No. We never sell user data.
